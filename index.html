Tagum Dialysis Center 97% complete, to offer free services

January 20, 2024

THE City Government of Tagum reported that the Diagnostic and Dialysis Center at the City Hall Grounds is now 97 percent complete. According to the latest update from the Department of Public Works and Highways (DPWH), the center is 97.01 percent finished.
